Our unique features for your unique needs

Guaranteed improvement

Champion Cubers' students have quickly seen major improvements in times and understanding of the puzzle, making them ready for anything from talent-shows to tournaments.

Experienced curriculum

Champion Cubers' curriculum has been used to teach many children how to speedcube. Some even learn how to solve the entire 3x3x3 cube within 4 hours!

Developing real-world thinking skills

Champion Cubers trains speedcubing skill based on the understanding of spatial thinking and mathematical concepts, not just rote memorization.

Multiply your mastery

Champion Cubers can teach you how to master all NxN cubes (2x2, 3x3...), puzzles like the pyramix and megamix, and non-WCA puzzles.

Make friends along the way

Champion Cubers' group programs like camps, club classes, or teams allow students to interact with others that share a hobby, making the experience extra-enjoyable.

Unrivaled resources

Champion Cubers equips each student with top-quality resources such as hardware, simulators, and custom reference material to boost the performance from learning and practice.

Playing by the rules

Champion Cubers' syllabus follows the World Cubing Association rules and guidelines for official practice based on professional competitions.

Elite training

Champion Cubers' founder and trainer, Soumil Goyal, has over 6 years of speedcubing experience to help you reach your goals while making sure you don't fall into beginner traps and bad habits.
