About Champion Cubers

I am on a mission to make speedcubing more accessible, widespread, and fun for everyone; learn more about myself and my mission here.

Hello, I am high schooler Soumil Goyal and I would like to tell you about my academy, Champion Cubers.

When I was 9, I received a Rubik's Cube as a gift. After getting close to solving the puzzle during 3 months of trial and error, I realized that the internet had solutions that I could use. A while later, using the internet and countless YouTube tutorials, I was able to solve the cube!

From then till now, getting better and learning more about Rubik's Cubes had only one obstacle for me: finding and learning the effective skills. Wherever I looked, websites and videos were filled with ads, scams, incomplete, and incorrect information. Due to this, even after many years of pracrice, I have not improved as much I would have liked to. That is why I started Champion Cubers, to teach kids how to solve the Rubik's Cube and many other puzzles through an experienced curriculum with proper guidance. Champion Cubers has delivered stunning results to every student that has participated and you will be able to reach your goals with Champion Cubers as well.

You can participate in a wide variety programs like camps, lessons, advanced training and more!

You can also master cracking the cubing code with Champion Cubers as I know I would have loved to when I first got my cube.
